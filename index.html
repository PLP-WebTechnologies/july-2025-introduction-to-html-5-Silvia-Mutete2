Compassionate Care, Close to Home

Kiriri Community Hospital provides comprehensive, patient‑centered care for families in Mwihoko and the surrounding areas. Our clinicians work as a team to ensure safe, high‑quality treatment from admission to recovery.

Our Core Services

24/7 Emergency Care

Rapid triage and treatment for urgent and life‑threatening conditions.

Outpatient & Family Medicine

Checkups, chronic disease management, and preventive screenings.

Maternity & Newborn

Comprehensive antenatal, delivery, and postnatal care in a family‑friendly environment.

Pediatrics

Child‑focused care from routine immunizations to specialist referrals.

Surgical Services

Elective and emergency procedures with safety‑first protocols.

Pharmacy

On‑site pharmacy with medication counseling and refills.
